# UA EdTech Lead Research Playbook

Цель sprint 1: 100 лидов за 3 дня, 200-250 first touches за 14 дней.

## Good-fit signals

- На сайте есть "Записатись на консультацію", "Безкоштовний пробний урок", "Залишити заявку", "Дізнатися вартість".
- Цена скрыта или зависит от консультации.
- Есть sales/admissions вакансии.
- Команда 10+ человек.
- Есть paid traffic: Meta/Google, retargeting, активный Instagram.
- Видны CRM/IP-телефония в вакансиях: Binotel, Ringostat, Stream, KeyCRM, amoCRM, Bitrix24.
- High-ticket или recurring модель.

## Bad-fit signals

- Marketplace репетиторов.
- Free/non-profit платформа.
- Solo creator без отдела продаж.
- Low-ticket self-serve checkout.
- Нет звонка/консультации до оплаты.

## Priority categories

1. Professional / business schools
2. IT / coding schools
3. English / language schools
4. Paid NMT / distance schools, seasonally
5. Children coding / teens, later

## Scoring

Fit score:

- 5 = perfect ICP, sales/admissions team, high-ticket/recurring
- 4 = good ICP, some details need verification
- 3 = possible fit
- 2 = weak fit
- 1 = drop

Trigger score:

- 5 = hiring sales, recent launch, founder posting about conversion, funding/news
- 4 = active in last 30 days
- 3 = active company, no trigger
- 2 = inactive profile
- 1 = drop

Priority score = fit + trigger.

- 8-10 = Priority A
- 6-7 = Priority B
- 4-5 = Priority C
- <4 = drop
