## 10. Methodology

Each call was reviewed across funnel stages:

- contact / rapport
- qualification
- presentation to need
- objection handling
- close / next step
- CRM / follow-up

Value at Risk is calculated conservatively from average deal value, observed pattern frequency and estimated recoverability. It is a prioritization estimate, not guaranteed revenue.
